About Us - SarkarSaathi.org

SarkarSaathi.org ko Delhi ke 2 bhai Alok Mohan Sharma aur Maddy ne banaya hai. Humara maksad hai har Indian ko sarkari yojana aur documents ka sahi aur aasan guide dena.

Hum khud dekha hai ki Aadhaar, PAN, Ration Card ke liye log kitna bhatakte hai, dalalon ko paise dete hai. Isliye humne 60+ tools ek jagah jama kiye hai, har tool ke saath official .gov.in link diya hai taaki aap bina kisi dalal ke apna kaam kar sake.

Hamari Kahani: Alok ne 2024 me dekha ki uske pados me ek aunty ko Ration Card ke liye 2000 Rs dena pada. Tabhi socha ki ek aisi site banaye jo 100% free ho, Hindi me ho, aur official link de. Maddy ne design aur development me madad ki. Aaj 11,000+ log roz hamari site use karte hai.
