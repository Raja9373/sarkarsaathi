Privacy Policy - SarkarSaathi.org

Last Updated: 22 July 2026 | Owner: Alok Mohan Sharma, Delhi

1. Information Collection

SarkarSaathi.org does not collect, store or ask for any personal data like Aadhaar number, PAN, bank details. All tools redirect to official .gov.in websites. We use localStorage only for visitor counter starting 11111. No login required.

2. Cookies & Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can opt-out via Google Ad Settings. We use DART cookies. Third-party vendors including Google use cookies to serve ads.

3. Log Data

Like many sites, we collect log data: IP, browser, pages visited, time. Used only for analytics and visitor counter. Counter starts from 11111 and increments in real time for display only. This data is AdSense safe and not linked to personal identity.

4. External Links

All 60+ tools link to official .gov.in, .nic.in, UIDAI, NSDL, IHMCL websites: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in, pmkisan.gov.in, voters.eci.gov.in, beneficiary.nha.gov.in, eshram.gov.in, parivahan.gov.in, gst.gov.in etc. We are not responsible for content on those official sites.

5. Data Deletion & Contact

Since we store no personal data, deletion request not needed. Still if you have concern, email contact@sarkarsaathi.org (Cloudflare verified forward to alokmohansharma.delhi@gmail.com). We reply within 24 hours.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Our site provides general government scheme info only.

7. Security

Site served over HTTPS via Vercel. No database, no personal data storage, hence minimal risk.
