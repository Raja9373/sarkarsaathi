3. Log Data: Vercel Analytics aur Cloudflare ke through basic visitor count (jaise total visitors counter) store hota hai, jo 11111 se start hota hai aur real visits pe badhta hai.

4. External Links: Hamari site par sabhi links official .gov.in websites par le jate hai. Unki privacy policy alag hogi.

5. Contact: Agar aapko apna data delete karwana hai toh contact@sarkarsaathi.org par mail kare, 24 hours me delete kar denge.
