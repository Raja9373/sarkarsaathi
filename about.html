About SarkarSaathi.org

SarkarSaathi.org was started in 2024 by two brothers from Delhi - Alok Mohan Sharma and Maddy. Story simple hai: hamari pados ki aunty ko ration card ke liye 1500 rupaye dene pade agent ko. Tab laga, ye kaam to free me hota hai, bas sahi link nahi milta logo ko.

We decided to make one place where all 60+ government services links are at one place, with clear steps in Hindi + English, official .gov.in links only, no fees, no data collection.

Today 11,000+ users daily use SarkarSaathi. We provide tools like PM Kisan, Ayushman Bharat, Ration Card, e-Shram, PAN, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Birth Certificate, EPF Balance, FASTag, ABHA, GST Registration, MSME, Bhulekh etc. Each with 4 required documents and 4 easy steps.

We are a private website, not government. All links go to official government websites. We only guide, we don't create documents. Our mission: Free, Fast, Genuine government information for every Indian.
